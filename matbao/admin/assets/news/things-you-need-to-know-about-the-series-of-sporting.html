Things you need to know about the series of sporting events “RACE TO MIEN TRUNG

2020 is a year full of difficulties and challenges because the Covid-19 pandemic takes place around the world. In addition, the double impacts from storms, floods and natural disasters continuously occurred on the central land, causing great damage to people and property.

But the spirit of mutual solidarity of the Vietnamese is shown in the most powerful way across the country, very emotional and proud.

It’s the reason that “RACE TO MIEN TRUNG” (Race to Central Vietnam) was chosen to become the key message of this series of sports events, as an encouragement from the organizing committee to ignite and spread the positive values to the fan clubs and the Vietnamese in general.

In line with the spirit, the Announcement Ceremony of two major sport events, which took place in December with the aim of raising runds through a close auction donated by many firms, and 10% from the ticket sales to see the Racing Festival and the items on display at Tan Son Nhat Golf Course in Ho Chi Minh City and at the Vietnam Racing Academy in the SVIP Lounge.

December 17, 2020, organizing committee will announce the auction results at the Sponsor Praise event, the sponsor and the team have won the two events above. The entire auction proceeds will accompany the OPEN ARM Foundation to build libraries for the provinces in the Central region.

Specifically about the chain of events included:

December 8th – 9th, 2020

The 2nd TASMANIA Golf Championship will take place with the participations from 50 largest golf clubs in the South, including teams from Nha Trang, Lam. Dong, Binh Thuan, Dong Nai, Vung Tau, Binh Duong and major golf clubs in Ho Chi Minh City with a total of 650 golfers. So far, the golf tournament has gathered many valuable Hole In One sponsors, such as: 4 luxury cars sponsored by Vinfast, Noble Klasse, Huyen Trang Industrial Company; a villa by Novaworld Phan Thiet, an apartment by the Wyndham Soleil Da Nang project and a total value of “Hole In One” prizes is up toVND 40 billion.

In particular, the organizers also received the sponsorship of Ping and Lecoq Sportif brands fully sponsoring prizes for individuals and teammates up to more than VND 750 million and Vietnam Racing Academy also sponsors this series of events to increase the value of the two brands and raise funds to support the people of Central Vietnam, this year’s event was officially broadcast live on December 9 on HTV broadcasts and livestream, livescore on online means.

The championship team in the 2020 season will be sponsored a golf tour including Golfing and Hotel at Cape Wickham golf course in Australia, the runner-up and third class teams will be sponsored a golf tour and yacht tour in Vietnam.

December 12, 2020

The biggest Sports Racing Festival in Vietnam will be held according to VMA standard – the only ASN of International Motor Federation, at Dai Nam racetrack gathered Professional Race has been granted VMA Racing license with expected scale of more than 20,000 participants; in which 5,000 VIP guests from enterprises in different fields such as VinFast, Motul, Diageo, Mortlach, SonKim Group, Reviv, …

The program is a unique combination between the racing festival with “Colorful music entertainment festival, present for the first time in Vietnam. The highlight of this event lies in the dramatic tough competitions from professional and semi-professional athletes from many competitive disciplines: large displacement motorcycles, Cars, Go-karts; with displays of excellent skill techniques: Stunt, flyboard and drifting. In particular, the participation of contestants – judges from Rap Viet & King of Rap program (Karik, De Choat, Yuno Bigboy, …) and professional DJs (DJ Mie, DJ Vinjaz, DJ Lai Thanh Huong , …) promises to bring explosive moments with attractive and passionate performances to the audience.

These are two major events in a series of professional events that take place regularly in the future in order to create more values for the community.

Let’s support “RACE TO CENTRAL” in particular, and raise the level of the country’s SPORT spirit in general.
